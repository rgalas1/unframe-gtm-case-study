Strategic AE Market Map

Having navigated the exact Series B inflection point while scaling the GTM function at Shapr3D, I understand the blend of enterprise maturity and startup scrappiness this stage demands. This shortlist maps ten candidates who align with the specific complexities of selling Unframe's AI-first transformation into majore enterprise orgs.

01. Search Strategy

Reading Between the Lines

While a specific territory was not defined, I went for a targeted geographic strategy focusing on major enterprise hubs. Proximity to Fortune 500 HQs and tier-one travel infrastructure is critical for executing complex, multi-threaded deal cycles.

Beyond geography, I evaluated candidates against the core elements required to thrive in this specific GTM motion, prioritizing five critical markers:

  • Stage-Relevant Scaling: Cross-referencing tenure against historical company growth phases to identify reps who navigated the ambiguity of an evolving GTM playbook and understand what good looks like when scaling.
  • Enterprise Deal Mechanics: The ability to move beyond technical evaluators and consistently build consensus among C-suite economic buyers on complex, $250K+ ACV deals.
  • Greenfield Pipeline Generation: Demonstrated history of manufacturing pipeline from scratch as an "early GTM hire" or regional builder, without relying on inbound engines or brand pull.
  • Outcome-Led Translation: Backgrounds in deep tech, data, or AI that require understanding customer pain in this area and translating highly complex capabilities into clear, multi-million dollar business ROI.
  • Ambiguity Tolerance: The capacity to guide enterprise accounts from initial problem discovery through to POCs and platform adoption while the internal playbook is still being written.
02. Evaluation Framework

Calibration & Matching

The candidates presented are all capable of succeeding at Unframe. The following scoring scale reflects the nuanced incrementality of their fit based on current career trajectory and immediate readiness for this exact stage.

Rating: 1

Strong Baseline Fit. Masters of the $250k+ technical sale within established enterprise data/AI giants. They have elite deal mechanics, though they may require a brief transition period to re-adapt to a greenfield environment.

Rating: 2

The Scaling Hunter. Combines deep technical enterprise mechanics with verified experience navigating high-growth, evolving GTM motions. They have successfully built regional pipelines with minimal brand support.

Rating: 3

The 0-to-1 Architect. The perfect storm of Unframe’s criteria. They possess native AI/Data domain expertise, command $1M+ quotas, and have explicitly operated as founding AEs, proving they manufacture pipeline from absolute zero.
